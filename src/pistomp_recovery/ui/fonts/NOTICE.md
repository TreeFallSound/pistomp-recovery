This font is the "Mx" (mixed outline + bitmap) variant of the IBM VGA 8x16
typeface — pixel-crisp at its native 16px size and the basis of the QBASIC /
DOS-era look of the recovery UI. It is shipped here as an independent,
separately-licensed asset (an aggregate alongside this AGPL-licensed program);
its CC BY-SA 4.0 terms apply to the font file itself.
